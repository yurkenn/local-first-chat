Why Lotus?

Built for privacy.
Designed for you.

Every feature in Lotus is designed with your security and experience in mind.

End-to-End Encrypted

Messages are encrypted on your device before they leave. Not even we can read them.

Local-First

Your data lives on your device. No central server storing your conversations.

Offline Support

Keep chatting even without internet. Messages sync automatically when you're back online.

Voice Channels

Crystal-clear encrypted voice chat with noise suppression and echo cancellation.

Cross-Platform

Available as a desktop app for macOS, Windows, Linux — and in your web browser.

Open Source

Fully open-source and auditable. Trust through transparency, not promises.
